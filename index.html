About Tiny-Tim's Tank

Previous Next

Tiny-Tim's Tank is a wonderous world where automation and life go hand in hand. Tiny-Tim, a Siamese Fighting Fish, is the embodiment of experience gathered from an environment designed to specifically to meet his needs. Tiny-Tim's Tank automates a lifestyle which is most suited to him based on his species and breed.
